If it is important, someone else is probably already working on it, too.

Unique ideas are seldom (if ever) unique. Every idea is built on the ideas of others and therefore claiming unique authorship is a questionable practice. Make the goal the discovery and curation of good ideas, the creation of good outcomes.

Claiming authorship of ideas that others also claim authorship over leads to a kind of fraudulent feeling.
