- Identify a topic for investigation
- Prepare a list of questions 
	- better: maybe a [[Dialog Map]]
- Prepare a method for recording the interview
- Determine criteria for selecting interviewees
- Identify people you will interview
- Schedule the interview (time, place, channel)
- Greet and set expectations for the interview
- Obtain (and document) consent
- Start with easy questions, move on to hard questions
	- better: follow the natural flow of the conversation, but be strategic about steering toward the topics you are most focused on addressing
- Listen actively
- Take good notes
- remember to say thank you

Cautions
- choose a distraction-free environment
- Don't lead the interview; don't put words in the interviewee's mouth
- Resist analysis
