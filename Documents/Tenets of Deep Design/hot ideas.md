A hot idea is one that comes through a flash of inspiration, apparently from nowhere. The thrill of discovery is heightened as observable patterns that support the idea manifest. The idea grows, bursting into existence, and the designer is not able to think about anything else. Hot ideas are explosive... and sometimes short-lived.

Hot ideas allow for the presence of intuition, and  [[Accept intuition as valid thought]].

Further, [[Intuition is acknowledged as a pre-linguistic way of thinking]], giving the designer access to deeper sources of knowledge that do not depend on language for expression.

Hot ideas come from the [[default mode network]] in the brain

Hot ideas are risky because they are often the result of [[Beware the Comforts of Bias]] and [[Acknowledge your ideology]]

beware of [[the $100 ferrari]], [[Beware of hidden impossibilities]]. Beware of out-of-control maxima
