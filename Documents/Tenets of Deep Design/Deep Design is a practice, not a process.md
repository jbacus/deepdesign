Deep Design is a practice, not a process. It does not have a beginning, or an end. Rather, every day and in every way the designer deepens their practice and improves their output.

[[Muses over methods]] not [[Methods over Muses]]
Deep desig is practiced by human designers who commit to drive innovation and problem solving every day, not just in the pursuit of some singular goal. The [[Expects end-user development: humans as active design participant for full lifecycle (Stewart brand)]]
[[Design Thinking]] is loved by corporates who favor predictable results over catastrophic failure or breakout innovation. Consider the [[Permanent Notes/Streetlight Problem]]
