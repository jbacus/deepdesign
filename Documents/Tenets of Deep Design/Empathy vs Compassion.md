Design thinking talks about “empathy” but it feels shallow. Not so much a desire to make people’s lives better as it is a method for understanding how to mine their needs for profit.

What can Vikas share about a practice of compassion, in the Indian tradition?

You have to actually care about people, in your heart. With practice, this becomes more natural, more instinctive. Pretending you care eventually is bad for both parties in the relationship.
